arcusx-chrono

Version: @version@ (This version is a production release with reduced feature set.)

arcusx-chrono is a package of day, month and year utilities for implementing business related date calculations. Its main goals are:

  • simplicity
  • reliable behaviour
  • integration with java.util.Date and Calendar
